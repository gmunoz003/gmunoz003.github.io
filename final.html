Travel Joe

Travel with a cup of Joe! Travel Joe, is a coffee shop that provides an experience that truly lasts a lifetime. Our shop offers a calm and humble environment fused with Honduran origin. The Munoz Family originally launched the company in hopes of helping young children in Central American find their calling and discover a better life. Today, Travel Joe, created mission trips to a variety of countries around the world and has donated thousands of dollars to children everywhere. If you're looking for a cup of coffee to change your morning and inspire your life, heres Travel Joe!
